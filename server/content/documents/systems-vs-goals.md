# Systems vs. Goals

Goals are for direction. Systems are for showing up.

A goal tells you what success looks like at the finish line. A system tells you what success looks like today — the thing you do whether you feel like it or not, whether you're winning or losing, whether the goal is twelve months away or three.

The people who do the most aren't the ones with the most ambitious goals. They're the ones whose default day already contains the work.
